More About Us

Immerse yourself in the wonders of nature as you embark on an exhilarating journey of kayaking, hiking, and bird watching. Whether you are a seasoned adventurer or a nature lover looking to connect with the great outdoors, our trail offers a diverse range of experiences to suit all levels of expertise. Our Goal at Pacific Trails Resort

Our mission is to offer a wonderful retreat that embraces the beauty of nature and encourage wellness. Nestled amidst breathtaking landscapes, we provide an escape from the harshness of life, combining the serenity of yoga, the coziness of yurts, and the thrill of hiking. Our experienced instructors guide you the yoga courses and our cozy yurts immerse you in the beauty of eco-friendly accommodations. Explore our extensive network of hiking trails, where every step reveals awe-inspiring vistas and connects you to the essence of the great outdoors. Experience a haven of tranquility, where the bond between mind, body, and nature is nurtured, leaving you with cherished memories and a renewed spirit.
